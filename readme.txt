Vanityvid turns your avatar into a video! 

== Description ==

Vanityvid turns avatars or profile pics into videos on your blog. Wherever your avatar shows, your Vanityvid will be attached to it.

Having videos instead of just plain profile pics increases the interaction and engagement of your blog, makes it more social, and it's also just plain cool.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `vanityvid.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Open the Vanityvid admin page by going to Settings -> Vanityvid on your dashboard.
1. Record your Vanityvid, confirm your email.
1. Start seeing your Vanityvid on your blog wherever your avatar appears!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How can I get a Vanityvid? =

On the Vanityvid admin page, you can record directly from your webcam, upload a file, or send a video file by email.

= Where will my Vanityvid appear? =

Your Vanityvid will appear wherever your Wordpress avatar appears on your blog. It will also appear on other blogs that installed this plugin.
